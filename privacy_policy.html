This privacy statement is created to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The Privacy Policy details if any of your personal information is collected and processed by Reacstor when you use the mobile application downloaded from Google Play Store.

Information Collection and Use

By default, our app doesn't collect any information.

When you request an icon, by default some data is gathered when you send the request. You can modify what you send in the email:

  • The email address which you send the request with;
  • The manufacturer, the model, product name, screen resolution, and Android version of your phone;
  • App version & Candybar version (the app dashboard);
  • Installed apps that you want icons of.

This information is solely used for the icon request and remains private. You're also fully controlling what data you send to us via email.

Crash Reporting, Analysis, and App Performance

We do not use any third-party integration besides Google Play Services and Google Firebase Crashlytics to collect app usage data or crash analytics. We only use the Android Vitals which help us understand and analyse Android app performance. Android Vitals provides us with the following information regarding crashes in the app:

  • Crash Log with the time of crash
  • App Version
  • Android Version
  • Device

We do not get any personal information in the ANRs (application not responding) & Crashes provided by Android Vitals.

For more information on what data is collected by Google from users who have opted in to automatically share usage and diagnostics data from a subset of Android devices and OS versions please visit: https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/6078260
